To My Bhuvan

— from Atchu, every word meant only for you —

My dear Bhuvan ❤️,

I don't know when it happened exactly, but somewhere along the way you became the person I think of first thing in the morning and last thing before I sleep. So I need you to know this, plainly and completely: no matter what happens in life, I will always be with you. In your happiest moments, I'll be the one laughing loudest beside you. In your hardest ones, I'll be the one holding your hand, refusing to let go.

"No matter how difficult the journey becomes, I want to be the person who holds your hand and walks beside you." ❤️

Bhuvan, life won't always be gentle with you. There will be days you feel tired, unsure, like the world is asking too much of you. On those days, I need you to remember something I believe with my whole heart — you are so much stronger than you give yourself credit for. Keep believing in yourself the way I believe in you. Keep working. Please don't ever let go of your dreams, because I haven't let go of mine for you.

"I don't love you only for your success; I love you for the person you are and the person you are becoming." 💕

I love you not for what you achieve, but for how gently you carry yourself through the world. Even on your worst days, when you doubt every part of yourself, I will be right here reminding you how rare and how capable you really are. Every late night you've pushed through, every fear you've quietly faced — I see it, and I am so proud of you.

"Behind every dream you chase, I will always be there — cheering for you, believing in you, and loving you." ❤️
✦

Don't be afraid of the obstacles ahead, Bhuvan. Every hard season is only shaping you into someone even stronger. Keep moving forward — I know, without a single doubt, that you can build the life you dream of. And I want a front-row seat to all of it.

"I may not be able to solve every problem in your life, but I promise you will never have to face them alone." 🌹

You are not alone, and you never will be while I'm around. I am here to listen when things get heavy, to encourage you when you forget your own strength, and to walk every single step of this journey right beside you — not behind you, not ahead of you, beside you.

"You are my favorite person, my biggest hope, and someone I will always be proud of." ❤️ "Some love stories are written in the stars — ours is written in the little moments, the late-night calls, and the way you make an ordinary day feel like home." 🌙 "If I had to choose between loving you and breathing, I'd use my last breath to say I love you." 💫 "Distance, time, and every difficulty are just tests to prove that some love is worth all of it — ours is, Bhuvan." 🌹
✦

Keep smiling. Keep believing in yourself the way you deserve to be believed in. Keep chasing every single dream, because I will be cheering for you louder than anyone. No matter where life takes us, my heart has already made its choice, and it chose you.

Today, tomorrow, and every day after — it's still you, Bhuvan. It will always be you.

With all my love,
your Atchu, forever ❤️
made with love, just for you 🌙✨
